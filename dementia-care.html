In-Home Dementia Care & Memory Care Services with Code Blue Care Companion


Few age-related conditions are as difficult as memory loss. Millions of people suffer from dementia and Alzheimer’s disease, impacting the lives of their families and friends. Dementia care at home brings physical and mental support to clients in the environment they are most comfortable in, providing a system of care and peace of mind for loved ones.

At-Home Dementia Care You Can Count On

Effective home care for dementia starts with a comprehensive home audit to provide the client with a safe and familiar setting.

Count on the memory care experts at Code Blue Care Companion to: >

  • Ensure safety in and around the home
  • Promote independence by modifying steps to an activity
  • Encourage client participation in their own care
  • Engage at the right time and in the right amount
  • Arrange closets and drawers to maximize independence
  • Monitor reactions to the environment, such as temperature or noise
  • Recognize when it's time for quiet and rest
  • Keep clients engaged in physical activity to promote circulation and muscle strength

How Dementia Home Care Services Help

That's why we work with you and your loved one to create a personalized plan of care that addresses their unique needs and always remembers that they are unique individuals.

Memory Care vs. Dementia Care: What's the Difference?

In-home memory care and dementia care share many similarities but have crucial differences:

Memory care

Professional support for individuals who need daily or weekly support managing daily tasks. Memory care can refer to in-home support services for dementia, Parkinson’s, Alzheimer’s, and other memory-related health concerns.

Dementia care

Usually describes care for seniors with more advanced cases. These individuals require assistance completing everyday tasks.

Professional Wellness Services for the Elderly FAQs

What is home care?

Home care encompasses a wide range of services that allow people to remain at home as they age, or while they recover from an injury, illness, or medical procedure. It also helps clients develop daily routines to cope with a chronic condition. Home care can include such services as laundry and light housekeeping, meal planning and preparation, transportation, and companionship.

What does in-home care provide?

In-home care assists those who need a hand with activities of daily living, such as dressing, grooming, and meal preparation. In-home care also provides the client’s family with peace of mind, knowing that their loved one has someone looking out for their best interests when family members can’t be there themselves.

What is home health care?

Home health care services place more emphasis on the medical aspects of a client’s home care needs. The home health care services provided by your local Homewatch CareGivers may include medication management, transportation to appointments, and communicating changes in health with family members and medical providers.

Who qualifies for home health care services?

There are many situations when individuals would benefit from home care services. Anyone dealing with a medical condition or injury could qualify for home health care services. Contact your local code blue CareGivers to discuss your home care needs.
