Home Wellness Care Services for Seniors & the Elderly with Code Blue Care Companion


Finding the right personal caregiver is about more than just completing daily tasks. At Code Blue Care Companion, we take pride in pairing seniors with the perfect home companion who can connect on a personal and caring level. Our home companions create a personalized plan of care, shaped by experience and training, and informed by valuable input from you and your loved one. Together, we promote physical safety and well-being while supporting emotional health.

What Is Wellness Care?

Elder wellness care is a home-based service designed to improve the lives of seniors living at home. Our wellness caregivers provide close connections that encourage seniors to stay active physically and mentally. From playing favorite card games to reliving treasured memories, personal caregivers offer warm social contact that combats loneliness, depression, and inactivity.

Personal companions for the elderly lend a listening ear and help with preventative care, ranging from preparing healthy meals to encouraging mentally and physically stimulating activities that support long-term health and vigor.

Other common wellness caregiver duties include:

  • Light housekeeping
  • Personal hygiene tasks, such as bathing and grooming
  • Medication reminders
  • Errands
  • Medical appointments
  • And much more

Elder Wellness Services We Offer

One size doesn’t fit all, especially when caring for seniors. Code Blue Care Companion offers flexible wellness care options to positively impact the health, happiness, and safety of our valued clients. Learn about the range of wellness care services we offer.

Social Wellness Care

Isolation is a health concern many face later in life. Whether due to a disability that limits mobility or geographic separation from loved ones, a lack of social connection has physical and mental consequences. A wellness caregiver offers consistent engagement and helps your loved one remain connected to friends and family.


Preventative Wellness Care

Home companions for the elderly can substantially reduce the risk factors of physical impairment. Issues that commonly contribute to impairment include:

  • Malnutrition
  • Weight fluctuations
  • Decreased stamina
  • Reduced muscle mass

Emotional Support, Comfort, & Friendship

Being social is essential at any age and helps maintain good mental health. Unfortunately, many seniors are at risk of isolation, which can lead to feelings of emptiness and depression. These emotions are often worsened after the loss of a spouse or friend or changes in their ability to socialize. By spending time with a caregiver, your loved one will have a meaningful social connection who can partake in activities they enjoy. These activities bring joy and mental stimulation that seniors need to stay healthy and vital.

Dignity and Independence

Wellness care for elders empowers older adults to live with dignity and without the concern of burdening their loved ones as they age. They maintain as much independence as possible, enjoy the company of a great friend, and don’t have to uproot their lives to move into an unfamiliar environment – which can lead to anxiety, fear, or depression.

Practical Assistance with Everyday Activities

Housework and daily living activities become more difficult with age. Meal prep, laundry, housekeeping, grocery shopping, remembering to take medication, and running errands become more challenging and even risky for seniors to handle alone. A companion caregiver helps with all these tasks and more.

Respite Relief for Family Caregivers

Primary caregivers sometimes need to balance caring for their loved ones and focusing on their own goals and obligations. With respite care from Code Blue Care Companion, you get professional, caring attendance for your loved one by someone qualified to care for their specific needs.

Bring Care Home with Code Blue Care Companion

Like a true companion, the trained, background-checked, and insured professional you hire from Code Blue Care Companion is there for your loved one – to care for, offer support, and help fill the void of what might otherwise be lonely hours. Working with a caregiver also makes the time you spend with your loved one less stressful and more satisfying.

Professional Wellness Services for the Elderly FAQs

What is non-medical elderly wellness care?

Most wellness care duties are considered non-medical wellness care. These duties include light housework, running errands, and social activities with seniors.

Does Medicare pay for wellness care?

In most cases, Medicare does not cover costs associated with in-home caregivers. Check your policy to learn what services are covered.

How Much Does Wellness Care Cost?

Wellness care rates vary depending on the frequency of care provided. There are many ways to offset the cost of wellness care so your loved one doesn’t have to do without the social interaction they need to thrive.
