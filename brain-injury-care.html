Back-Up Care with Code Blue Care Companion


Code Blue Care Companion is Ready to Save the Day!

Sometimes, small emergencies and unexpected schedule changes disrupt your plans. When this happens, Code Blue Care Companion is ready to provide the quality back-up care you deserve! Whether you face a last-minute schedule change or your regular care provider is unavailable due to unforeseen circumstances, we can help. The same expertise and service you're accustomed to are available for a variety of home care needs for children and adults. Back-up care benefits may be offered through your employer and may cover the cost associated with care for dependent family members of all ages.


Home care isn’t only for those needing assistance due to health or mobility issues. For example, individuals working from home might need help keeping their children engaged throughout the day. The skilled and compassionate caregivers at our locally operated locations are ready to keep kids occupied with meaningful activities, prepare their meals, or take them outside for activities.


Our caregivers providing back-up child and adult care services undergo additional training. Rest assured, all caregivers and other members of our local care teams are insured and have undergone three levels of pre-employment checks: character, care expertise, and criminal background investigations.

We offer an array of care services customized for family members of all ages. Some of these back-up care solutions include:

  • Back-up and urgent care for children and adults
  • Prenatal and postnatal care for mothers
  • Care for children living with special needs
  • Surgical after-care for people of all ages

Frequently Asked Questions

What is home care?

Home care encompasses a wide range of services that allow people to remain at home as they age, or while they recover from an injury, illness, or medical procedure. It also helps clients develop daily routines to cope with a chronic condition. Home care can include such services as laundry and light housekeeping, meal planning and preparation, transportation, and companionship.

What does in-home care provide?

In-home care assists those who need a hand with activities of daily living, such as dressing, grooming, and meal preparation. In-home care also provides the client’s family with peace of mind, knowing that their loved one has someone looking out for their best interests when family members can’t be there themselves.

What is home health care?

Home health care services place more emphasis on the medical aspects of a client’s home care needs. The home health care services provided by your local Homewatch CareGivers may include medication management, transportation to appointments, and communicating changes in health with family members and medical providers.

Who qualifies for home health care services?

There are many situations when individuals would benefit from home care services. Anyone dealing with a medical condition or injury could qualify for home health care services. Contact your local code blue CareGivers to discuss your home care needs.
