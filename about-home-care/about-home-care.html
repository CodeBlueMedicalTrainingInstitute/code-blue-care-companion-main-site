Frequently Asked Questions

What is home care?

Home care is a comprehensive service that enables individuals to stay in their homes while aging, recuperating from health issues, or managing chronic conditions. It includes a variety of services such as housekeeping, meal preparation, transportation, and companionship.

What does in-home care provide?

In-home care offers assistance with daily activities like dressing, grooming, and cooking. It also gives families the assurance that their loved ones are cared for when they cannot be present.

What is home health care?

Home health care focuses on medical needs at home, such as medication management, transport to medical appointments, and monitoring health changes, all coordinated with family and healthcare providers.

Who qualifies for home health care services?

Individuals with medical conditions or recovering from injuries may qualify for home health care services.

How do I get home care services?

Starting with Code Blue Care Companion is easy. Contact us to discuss your care needs, and we can often provide a caregiver on the same day.
