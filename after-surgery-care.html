After-Surgery Home Care Service with Code Blue Care Companion


Ensuring a Smooth and Comfortable Recovery with Code Blue Care Companion

The journey through surgery and its subsequent recovery can be daunting. Code Blue Care Companion is here to ease this transition with a strategic and compassionate home care plan, providing support for those who rely on you for their mobility and independence.


Comprehensive Post-Surgery Care Services:

  • Transportation for Medical Appointments

    • We ensure timely transportation to and from medical appointments, keeping your recovery on the right path.

  • Nutritious Meal Preparation

    • Our caregivers assist with meal preparation, ensuring you have the nutritious meals necessary for a swift recovery.

  • Light Housekeeping Duties

    • A tidy home environment is vital for recovery. Our team assists with light housekeeping to maintain a clean and safe living space.

  • Medication Management

    • We provide medication reminders and assist with prescription pick-ups, ensuring you stay on top of your medication schedule.

  • In-Home Post-Appointment Care

    • Our professionals help you adhere to post-appointment care instructions, including wound care and other physician-recommended practices.

  • Laundry Services

    • We offer laundry assistance to alleviate the physical strain of household chores during your recovery.

  • Assistance with Personal Care

    • Our caregivers provide support with personal care tasks, ensuring comfort and hygiene during your recovery.


Tailor your home care experience with our individual services or a comprehensive care package, customized to meet the specific needs of your post-surgery recovery.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is home care?

Home care encompasses a wide range of services that allow people to remain at home as they age, or while they recover from an injury, illness, or medical procedure. It also helps clients develop daily routines to cope with a chronic condition. Home care can include such services as laundry and light housekeeping, meal planning and preparation, transportation, and companionship.

What does in-home care provide?

In-home care assists those who need a hand with activities of daily living, such as dressing, grooming, and meal preparation. In-home care also provides the client’s family with peace of mind, knowing that their loved one has someone looking out for their best interests when family members can’t be there themselves.

What is home health care?

Home health care services place more emphasis on the medical aspects of a client’s home care needs. The home health care services provided by your local Homewatch CareGivers may include medication management, transportation to appointments, and communicating changes in health with family members and medical providers.

Who qualifies for home health care services?

There are many situations when individuals would benefit from home care services. Anyone dealing with a medical condition or injury could qualify for home health care services. Contact your local code blue CareGivers to discuss your home care needs.
